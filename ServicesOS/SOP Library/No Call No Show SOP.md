No Call No Show SOP

Category: Employee Management

Trigger
Employee misses shift without notice
Step 1: Verification
□ Attempt contact
□ Call employee
□ Text employee
□ Emergency contact if required
Step 2: Immediate Coverage
□ Review today's jobs
□ Review available staff
□ Reassign jobs
Step 3: Documentation
□ Record incident
□ Record communication attempts
□ Notify management
Completion Criteria
Jobs covered and incident documented

If I were designing a cleaning SOP, I would separate:

Every Visit
Dust furniture
Microfiber cloth
No polish
Periodic Maintenance
Furniture polish
Wood conditioning
Detailed wood care

For most homes, using something like Pledge every cleaning is usually unnecessary and can actually create buildup over time.

A schedule like:

Every 6 months

or

Twice per year

for furniture polishing is much more in line with what many homeowners actually need.

For your wife's business, you could structure it as:

Standard Clean

Includes:

Dusting
Wiping surfaces
Vacuuming
Bathrooms
Kitchen
Floors

Furniture gets dusted but not polished.

Seasonal Refresh Add-On

Every 6 months:

Furniture polishing
Wood conditioning
Ceiling fans
Baseboards
Window tracks
Behind furniture

That becomes a premium service.

Standard Clean

+$50

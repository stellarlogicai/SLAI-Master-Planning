Locked Out SOP

Category: Operations

Trigger
Crew cannot access property
Step 1: Verify Access
□ Verify address
□ Verify entry instructions
□ Verify lockbox instructions
□ Verify gate codes
Step 2: Contact Customer
□ Call customer
□ Send message
□ Wait required policy period
Step 3: Escalate
□ Notify dispatcher
□ Notify manager if required
Step 4: Resolution
□ Gain access
□ Reschedule
□ Cancel per policy
Documentation
□ Record wait time
□ Record communication attempts
□ Record outcome
Completion Criteria
Issue resolved and documented

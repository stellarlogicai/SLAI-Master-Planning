Employee Call-Out SOP

Category: Employee Management

This one is extremely important because every company deals with it.

Trigger
Employee unable to work scheduled shift
Step 1: Receive Notification
□ Record call-out
□ Record reason
□ Record timestamp
Step 2: Assess Impact
□ Review today's jobs
□ Review assigned customers
□ Review crew assignments
Step 3: AI Recommendation
□ Review replacement recommendations
□ Review certification requirements
□ Review travel distances
□ Review workloads
Step 4: Reassign
□ Assign replacement
□ Update schedule
□ Notify employee
Step 5: Customer Communication

Only if necessary.

□ Notify customer of delays
□ Notify customer of crew changes
Completion Criteria
All affected jobs reassigned

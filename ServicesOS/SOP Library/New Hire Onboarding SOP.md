New Hire Onboarding SOP

Category: Employee Management

Purpose:
Standardize employee onboarding.

Trigger
New employee hired
Step 1: Employee Setup
□ Create employee profile
□ Assign employee ID
□ Add contact information
□ Add emergency contact
□ Upload required documents
Step 2: Payroll Setup
□ Complete tax forms
□ Verify payment method
□ Verify direct deposit
□ Verify payroll profile
Step 3: App Access
□ Create login
□ Assign permissions
□ Verify app access
□ Verify mobile device setup
Step 4: Training Assignment
□ Assign Orientation
□ Assign Chemical Safety
□ Assign Residential Fundamentals
□ Schedule completion deadline
Step 5: Certification Tracking
□ Verify training completion
□ Verify quiz completion
□ Verify supervisor approval
Completion Criteria
Employee fully onboarded

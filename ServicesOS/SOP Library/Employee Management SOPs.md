What we just finished was:

Training = How to do the job

Checklist = What to do during the job

Now we're building:

SOP = What to do when something goes wrong

This is one of the biggest differences between a small cleaning company and a scalable company.

Without SOPs:

Problem Happens
↓
Call Owner
↓
Owner Makes Decision

With SOPs:

Problem Happens
↓
Follow SOP
↓
Escalate If Needed
↓
Problem Resolved

That is how you eventually support hundreds of employees.

B.2 Employee Call-Out SOP

□ Record reason
□ Record timestamp

□ Review today's jobs
□ Review assigned customers
□ Review crew assignments
Step 3: AI Recommendation
□ Review replacement recommendations
□ Review certification requirements
□ Review travel distances
□ Review workloads
Step 4: Reassign
□ Assign replacement
□ Update schedule
□ Notify employee
Step 5: Customer Communication

Only if necessary.

□ Notify customer of delays
□ Notify customer of crew changes
Completion Criteria
All affected jobs reassigned

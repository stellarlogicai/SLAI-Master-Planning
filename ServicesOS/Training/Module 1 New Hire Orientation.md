Understand customer service expectations.

Be prepared to perform assigned cleaning jobs safely and professionally.

Slide 1: Welcome To The Team
Title
Welcome To The Team
Content

Welcome to our cleaning team.

Every employee plays an important role in delivering exceptional service to our customers.

Our goal is simple:

Deliver high-quality cleaning services
Treat customers with respect
Work safely and professionally
Support fellow team members

Your success helps our customers, our company, and your fellow employees succeed.

Image Suggestion
Professional cleaning team
Company branded image
Slide 2: Your Role
Title
Understanding Your Role
Content

As a cleaning professional, you are responsible for:

Arriving on time
Following assigned schedules
Completing cleaning tasks
Communicating professionally
Reporting issues promptly
Representing the company positively

Customers often judge the company based on their interactions with you.

Key Takeaway
You are the face of the company.
Slide 3: Appearance & Uniform Standards
Title
Appearance & Uniform Standards
Content

Employees are expected to:

Wear approved uniforms
Maintain personal hygiene
Wear clean clothing
Display identification when required
Wear appropriate footwear

Avoid:

Offensive clothing
Excessive fragrances
Unsafe footwear
Key Takeaway
Professional appearance builds customer trust.
Slide 4: Professional Conduct
Title
Professional Conduct
Content

Always:

Be respectful
Be courteous
Be honest
Remain professional

Never:

Argue with customers
Use inappropriate language
Enter unauthorized areas
Discuss customer information
Key Takeaway
Professionalism is expected at all times.

Title
The Standard Job Workflow
Content

Every cleaning job follows the same process:

Travel To Job
↓
Arrive
↓
Start Job
↓

↓

↓

↓
Take After Photos
↓
Customer Signature
↓
Complete Job

Following the workflow helps ensure consistency and quality.

Key Takeaway
Never skip workflow steps.
Slide 6: Attendance & Time Tracking
Title
Attendance & Time Tracking
Content

Employees are expected to:

Clock in accurately

Record lunch periods
Clock out at the end of the day

If you will be late:

Notify management immediately
Key Takeaway
Accurate time tracking protects both employees and the company.

Communicate with management

The employee app is your primary work tool.
Slide 8: Customer Communication
Title
Customer Communication
Content

When interacting with customers:

Notify management
Follow company procedures

Key Takeaway

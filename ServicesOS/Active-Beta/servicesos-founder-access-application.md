## Purpose

This document defines the Founder Access application form for ServicesOS.

The application should filter for good-fit cleaning businesses before Jamie gives a live demo or does onboarding.

Core rule:

```text
The application is not a generic contact form.
It is a qualification filter.
```

## Form Goal

The form should answer:

- Is this a real cleaning business?
- Do they have recurring jobs?
- Do they have helpers or plan to?
- Do they have the workflow pain ServicesOS solves?
- Are they willing to use an early founder-supported version?
- Are they willing to give feedback?
- Can they realistically pay after the trial?

## Required Questions

Use these questions:

1. Business name
2. Location
3. Your name
4. Best contact method
5. What type of cleaning work do you do?
6. How many helpers/employees do you have?
7. How many recurring clients do you currently have?
8. Do you do move-out, real-estate prep, or turnover cleans?
9. What tools do you use now?
10. What is your biggest admin headache?
11. Are you willing to test an early founder-supported version?
12. Are you willing to give feedback during the first month?
13. Would $39–$49/month be realistic if ServicesOS saved time or reduced missed details?

## Optional Questions

Optional but useful:

- Do you currently use Jobber, ZenMaid, Launch27, BookingKoala, Housecall Pro, or another software platform?
- Do you need payroll right away?
- Do you need automatic texting right away?
- Do you need QuickBooks replacement right away?
- Do helpers need to use the system from their phone?
- Do you currently use before/after photos?
- Do you track deposits/final payments now?

## Disqualification Logic

Reject or mark “not ready” if:

- no recurring clients
- unwilling to pay anything
- unwilling to give feedback
- needs payroll immediately
- needs automatic SMS immediately
- needs full QuickBooks replacement immediately
- already happy with mature software
- wants custom software
- refuses to use it on real jobs

## Strong Fit Signals

Strong fit if they say:

- they have weekly/biweekly/monthly recurring jobs
- they have 1–5 helpers
- they lose notes/photos in texts
- they use multiple disconnected tools
- they do move-out, real-estate prep, or turnover work
- helpers need clearer job instructions
- owner spends too much time after work handling admin
- they would try ServicesOS on a real job this month
- $39–$49/month is realistic if useful

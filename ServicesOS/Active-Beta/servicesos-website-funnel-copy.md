## Purpose

This document defines the low-cost website funnel and copy for ServicesOS Founder Access.

The website should not try to make ServicesOS look bigger than it is.

It should:

1. Explain the pain.
2. Show who ServicesOS is for.
3. Let people try an interactive fake-data demo.
4. Filter serious Founder Access applicants.

## Problem Section

```text
If your cleaning business is growing, the work starts spreading everywhere:

- customer notes in texts
- schedules in Google Calendar
- estimates in spreadsheets
- photos on phones
- payment status in Square, Cash App, Venmo, QuickBooks, Wave, or memory
- helpers asking what needs done
- owner cleaning up admin after work

ServicesOS brings the job workflow into one place.
```

## Who It Is For

```text
ServicesOS Founder Access is for small cleaning businesses that:

- have recurring clients
- have 1–5 helpers or plan to add helpers
- do residential, move-out, real-estate prep, or turnover cleans
- use texts, calendars, spreadsheets, paper, or payment apps
- want a simpler way to manage jobs
- are willing to give direct feedback
```

## Who It Is Not For

```text

- full payroll

- automatic SMS marketing

- a mature enterprise platform
```

## FAQ

### Is ServicesOS finished?

```text
ServicesOS is in early Founder Access. It is meant for good-fit cleaning businesses that understand it is early and are willing to give feedback.
```

### Does it replace QuickBooks?

```text
No. ServicesOS is focused on job workflow, not full accounting replacement. Accounting integrations or exports may come later.
```

### Does it handle payroll?

```text
No. Payroll is not part of the first version.
```

### Does it send automatic texts?

```text
Not in the first version. SMS adds cost and complexity, so ServicesOS is keeping that off until customers prove they need it.
```

### Can helpers use it?

```text
The goal is for helpers to have a simple field workflow: job list, job details, checklist, notes/photos, and complete job.
```

### What happens if I cancel?

```text
You should be able to export your core data. ServicesOS is not meant to trap your business.
```

### Why not just use Jobber or ZenMaid?

```text
Those tools may be right for some businesses. ServicesOS is for small cleaning owner-operators who want a simpler founder-supported workflow and are not already happy with mature software.
```

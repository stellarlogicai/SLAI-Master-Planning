Project: Stellar Logic AI / ServicesOS  
Status: Active after wife beta blockers are fixed  
Priority: Founder-led, zero/low-budget outreach  
Last Updated: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

This document stores low-pressure outreach scripts for ServicesOS Founder Access.

Use these only after wife beta is stable enough to show ServicesOS to outside prospects.

## Tone Rules

Use a tone that is:

- honest
- low-pressure
- founder-led
- not spammy
- not salesy
- clear that ServicesOS is early
- clear that Jamie is looking for feedback and a few good-fit testers

Avoid:

- revolutionary
- AI-powered
- guaranteed
- replace all your tools
- limited time only
- fake urgency
- enterprise-grade
- autonomous

## 10-Minute Discovery Call Opening

```text
Thanks for taking a few minutes. I’m not going to hard-sell you. I’m trying to see whether ServicesOS is actually useful for small cleaning owners.

I’ll ask how you manage jobs now. If it sounds like a fit, I can show you the demo. If it does not, I’ll tell you honestly.
```

## 10-Minute Discovery Questions

Ask:

1. How many recurring clients do you have?
2. Do you work solo or have helpers?
3. How do you track your schedule?
4. How do you create estimates?
5. How do helpers know what needs done?
6. Where do you keep job notes, access notes, pet notes, or special instructions?
7. Do you take before/after photos?
8. How do you track deposit/final payment status?
9. What part of admin annoys you most?
10. Have you tried software before?
11. Would $39–$49/month be realistic if this helped?

## End Call If Not Fit

```text
Based on what you told me, I don’t think ServicesOS is the right fit yet. I’d rather be honest than waste your time.

If the product grows into what you need later, I can reach back out.
```

## Invite To Demo If Fit

```text
This sounds close to what ServicesOS is built for. The next step would be a 20-minute demo using fake cleaning data so you can see the workflow.
```

## 20-Minute Qualified Demo Flow

1. Restate their pain.
2. Show customer record.
3. Create estimate.
4. Schedule job.
5. Assign helper.
6. Show helper job view.
7. Complete checklist.
8. Add notes/photos.
9. Complete job.
10. Owner review.
11. Payment status.
12. Ask: “Would this help your real workflow or add extra work?”

## 45–60 Minute Onboarding Flow

Only for committed Founder Access users.

1. Confirm expectations.
2. Confirm limitations.
3. Create account/business profile.
4. Add/import customers.
5. Add helpers.
6. Add service types.
7. Create first estimate.
8. Schedule first recurring job.
9. Assign helper.
10. Walk through field workflow.
11. Set first-week check-in.
12. Agree on feedback process.

## Final Rule

```text
Interview many.
Demo few.
Onboard only serious fits.
```

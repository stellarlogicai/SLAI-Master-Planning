# ServicesOS Competitor Research

## Purpose

This file tracks competitors, alternatives, and market positioning for ServicesOS.

Goal:

```text
Understand the market without copying competitors.
```

ServicesOS should win by being:

- Easier to use
- Fairly priced
- More integrated
- Cleaner for small businesses
- Strong on employee workflow
- Strong on training
- Built from real operational pain

# Non-Software Competitors

Many small cleaning companies may currently use:

```text
Google Calendar
Spreadsheets
Text messages
Paper checklists
QuickBooks
Cash App/Venmo
Manual estimates
```

These are not traditional competitors, but they are the real current workflow.

ServicesOS must be easier than:

```text
"Just keep doing it manually."
```

---

# Positioning

ServicesOS is not trying to be the biggest enterprise system at launch.

ServicesOS should be:

```text
The easiest way for a small cleaning business to manage customers, estimates, scheduling, employee work, training, and payments from one place.
```

---

# Research Questions

- What are cleaning companies currently paying?
- Which tools do they hate?
- Which features do they actually use?
- What causes them to switch software?
- What keeps them from adopting software?
- What does a solo/small cleaning business need first?
- What does a 5-15 employee cleaning business need next?
- What pricing feels fair?

---

# Rule

Do not copy competitors feature-for-feature.

Use research to identify:

```text
Pain
Confusion
Pricing gaps
Workflow gaps
Customer complaints
```

Then build ServicesOS around real customer needs.

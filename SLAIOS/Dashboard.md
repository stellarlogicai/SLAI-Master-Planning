# SLAIOS Dashboard

Purpose: Founder command center.

Sections:
- Revenue (MRR, ARR, Profit, Expenses)
- Projects (status, progress, milestones)
- Customers (growth, churn, support)
- Development (bugs, deployments, test coverage)
- AI Suggestions (risks, opportunities, priorities)

Design Principles:
- Dashboard first, AI second
- One-screen company health
- Drill down into projects
- Highlight only actionable alerts

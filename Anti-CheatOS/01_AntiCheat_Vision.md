# Anti-Cheat Vision

## Mission
SLAI Anti-Cheat exists to protect competitive integrity through behavioral telemetry, explainable evidence, and human-reviewed enforcement.

Core principle:

```txt
Suspicious does not mean guilty.
Suspicious means investigate further.
```

## Product Positioning
Preferred term:

```txt
Real-time Competitive Integrity System
```

This is more than classic anti-cheat. It is behavioral intelligence for competitive games.

## Philosophy
Traditional anti-cheat often looks for known signatures, memory tampering, known cheat binaries, or kernel-level indicators. SLAI focuses on player behavior:

- Aim path
- Reaction time
- Recoil control
- Target switching
- Movement patterns
- Input timing
- Human-like inconsistency
- Entropy
- Micro-corrections

## Core Pipeline

```txt
Game telemetry
↓
Feature extraction
↓
Player baseline
↓
Human baseline
↓
Known cheat pattern comparison
↓
Risk scoring
↓
Investigation mode
↓
Evidence timeline
↓
Human review
↓
Studio decision
```

## Human-First Enforcement
AI can flag, explain, monitor, and recommend. AI should not automatically issue permanent bans without policy approval, strong evidence, and review.

## Final Principle
The strongest anti-cheat system is not the fastest to punish. It is the best at understanding behavior before acting.

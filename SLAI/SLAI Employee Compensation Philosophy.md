# SLAI Employee Compensation Philosophy

**Version:** 2.0

---

# Purpose

Compensation exists to:

* Reward contributions.
* Encourage growth.
* Retain exceptional employees.
* Share success.
* Build loyalty.

Employees should feel:

"I am valued here."

---

# Salary Philosophy

SLAI believes in:

* Competitive salaries.
* Transparent compensation.
* Sustainable payroll.
* Predictable raises.

Compensation should never:

* Be arbitrary.
* Create resentment.
* Endanger company stability.

---

# Annual Reviews

Every employee receives:

Annual:

* Performance Review
* Salary Review
* Career Discussion
* Goal Planning Session

Raises are expected.

Growth should be rewarded.

---

# Annual Raises

Employees meeting expectations:

2% - 4%

Annual Raise

---

Employees exceeding expectations:

5% - 8%

Annual Raise

---

Exceptional employees:

8% - 12%

Annual Raise

PLUS

Bonus

PLUS

Promotion discussion.

---

# Promotion Philosophy

Employees should know:

* Where they are.
* Where they can go.
* What skills are required.
* How to grow.

Career growth should be:

Clear.

Fair.

Achievable.

---

# Exceptional Employees

If an employee:

Creates value far beyond their compensation:

Compensate them.

Do not lose exceptional people over:

$5,000

$10,000

or

$20,000.

---

Exceptional people:

Create exceptional companies.

---

# Founder Compensation

Minimum:

$80,000/year

Comfortable:

$150,000/year

---

Raises:

Milestone Only.

Never emotional.

Never random.

Never at the expense of employees.

---

# Founder Sacrifice

If the company struggles:

Founder:

Raise = 0

Bonus = 0

---

Employees:

Protected whenever possible.

---

Founder sacrifices first.

Employees sacrifice last.

---

# Annual Bonus Pool

Target:

20%

of yearly profits.

---

Bonus pool rewards:

* Company milestones
* Team performance
* Exceptional contributions
* Long term commitment

---

Bonuses celebrate success.

Salary creates stability.

---

# Profit Sharing

Long term goal:

Implement profit sharing.

Purpose:

* Encourage ownership mentality.
* Reward long term commitment.
* Align company and employee goals.

---

# Phantom Equity

Reserved for:

* Key leaders.
* Long-term employees.
* Exceptional contributors.

Purpose:

Ownership-like rewards

WITHOUT

transferring company ownership.

---

# Real Equity

Real equity should be:

Rare.

Reserved for:

Extraordinary circumstances.

Founder ownership should remain protected whenever possible.

---

# Transparency

Employees deserve to know:

* Salary ranges.
* Raise criteria.
* Promotion paths.
* Bonus eligibility.

Nobody should wonder:

"Am I valued here?"

The answer should always be obvious.

---

# Final Principle

Employees are not expenses.

Employees are investments.

Invest in them.

Grow with them.

Celebrate their victories.

Because:

Their wins

are our wins.

# SLAI Hiring Standards

Version: 1.0

---

# Philosophy

Hire character first.

Teach skills second.

---

# Required Traits

Candidates should demonstrate:

* Honesty
* Curiosity
* Humility
* Ownership
* Creativity
* Courage
* Respect
* Desire to learn

---

# Red Flags

Immediate concerns:

* Dishonesty
* Arrogance
* Toxicity
* Blaming others
* Refusal to learn
* Disrespect

---

# Disagreement

SLAI values disagreement.

Employees should feel safe saying:

"I think this is wrong."

Provided they:

* Explain reasoning.
* Present evidence.
* Remain respectful.

---

# Founder Question

Can this person tell Jamie:

"I disagree."

and defend their position?

If no:

They may not be a good fit.

---

# Diversity of Thought

SLAI intentionally hires:

* Specialists
* Generalists
* Researchers
* Self taught builders
* Traditional experts
* Outside-the-box thinkers

Innovation requires multiple perspectives.

# SLAI Founder Handbook

**Version:** 1.0
**Founder:** Jamie Brown
**Company:** Stellar Logic AI (SLAI)

---

# Introduction

SLAI exists to create meaningful technology that improves the lives of customers, employees, and society.

Profit is important.

Profit allows us to:

* Build better products.
* Hire exceptional people.
* Support meaningful causes.
* Create opportunities for others.
* Continue innovating.

Profit is not the mission.

The mission is impact.

---

# Core Values

## Human First AI

Artificial Intelligence should enhance people, not replace them.

SLAI builds systems that:

* Improve employee productivity.
* Improve decision making.
* Reduce repetitive work.
* Empower businesses.

We do not pursue human replacement solely for short-term profit.

---

## Long-Term Thinking

Every decision should be evaluated by:

* Will this help customers?
* Will this help employees?
* Will this strengthen SLAI in 5 years?
* Would I be proud of this decision in 20 years?

Short-term gains should never compromise long-term trust.

---

## Integrity

Do the right thing.

Even if:

* It costs money.
* It delays growth.
* Nobody notices.

Integrity is not negotiable.

---

## Continuous Learning

SLAI encourages:

* Curiosity
* Experimentation
* Research
* Education
* Documentation

The company should never stop learning.

Neither should its founder.

# Founder Compensation Philosophy

## Minimum Salary

$80,000/year

Requirements:

* Company profitable.
* Emergency reserves maintained.
* Employees properly compensated.
* Founder duties fulfilled.

---

## Comfortable Salary

$150,000/year

Requirements:

* Stable operations.
* Mature leadership team.
* 12 month reserves.
* Founder not required for daily operations.

---

## Salary Rules

Founder salary increases:

* Only at milestones.
* Never because of emotion.
* Never at the expense of company stability.

---

## Founder Bonuses

Bonuses are encouraged.

Examples:

* Customer milestones.
* Revenue milestones.
* Product launches.
* Major company achievements.

Bonuses celebrate success.

Salary provides stability.

# Product Expansion Rules

New products require:

* Dedicated team
* Stable revenue
* Existing products performing well
* Emergency fund intact

Never launch products simply because:

"The founder got excited."

Ideas are infinite.

Resources are not.

---

# Vertical Expansion Rules

New verticals require:

* Existing vertical profitable
* Existing vertical stable
* Existing vertical growing
* Documentation complete

Expand deliberately.

Not emotionally.

---

# Investment Philosophy

Default answer:

No.

---

Investment will only be considered if:

* Founder retains control.
* Minority ownership only.
* No forced exit.
* No board control.
* Company values remain unchanged.
* Investor provides strategic value.

Money alone is not enough.

Freedom is valuable.

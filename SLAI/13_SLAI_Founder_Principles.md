# SLAI Founder Principles

## Leadership
- Employees before me
- Their wins are my wins
- Leadership serves
- Data over ego
- Respect over fear
- Morality over profits

## Wealth
Comfortable quiet life. Take care of family. Give back when possible.

## Innovation
Question assumptions. Outside-the-box thinking. Crazy ideas welcome.

## AI
AI assists. Humans decide. AI explains itself. Morality matters.

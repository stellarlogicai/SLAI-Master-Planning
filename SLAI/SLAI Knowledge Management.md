# SLAI Knowledge Management

Version: 1.0

---

# Philosophy

Knowledge belongs to the company.

Not individuals.

---

# Document Everything

Required:

* Architecture decisions
* SOPs
* Product roadmaps
* Research notes
* AI experiments
* Meeting summaries
* Incident reports

---

# Every Decision Must Answer

What changed?

Why?

Who approved it?

What problem does it solve?

---

# Forbidden Knowledge

Never allow:

"It works because Jamie said so."

or

"Only one person understands this."

---

# Research Archive

Every experiment:

* Hypothesis
* Dataset
* Method
* Results
* Conclusions
* Future Ideas

---

Failure is valuable.

Document failures.

Learn from failures.

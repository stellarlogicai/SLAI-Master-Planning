# Why SLAI Exists

SLAI was not founded to maximize profit. Profit keeps the mission alive, but profit is not the mission.

## We Believe
- Humans deserve better tools
- Employees deserve respect
- Customers deserve honesty
- AI should explain itself
- Suspicion deserves investigation
- Data informs decisions
- Morality matters

## Why We Build
The world has too many bad systems. SLAI exists to build intelligent systems that remove friction so humans can spend more time being human.

## Final Principle
Products will come and go. SLAI is built around principles. As long as those principles survive, SLAI survives.

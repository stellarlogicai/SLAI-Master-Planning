# Founder Exit Rules

Jamie may step back from daily operations when:

* Leadership team exists.
* Operations Manager established.
* Emergency fund reaches 12 months.
* Founder absence does not threaten operations.
* Customer experience remains excellent.

---

Jamie may become Chairman when:

* Vision matters more than daily involvement.
* Leadership consistently protects company values.

---

Jamie may appoint a CEO ONLY if:

That person:

* Believes in SLAI values.
* Protects employees.
* Protects customers.
* Thinks long term.
* Enhances people with AI.
* Prioritizes integrity.

---

# Founder Ownership Philosophy

Majority ownership should remain with the founder whenever possible.

Investment is allowed only if:

* Founder retains control.
* Vision remains unchanged.
* Employees are protected.
* Customers are protected.

Money alone is not enough.

Freedom matters.

# Future Features

## Save Draft

Allow respondents to save progress and finish later.

## Branching Logic

Show different questions depending on role or previous answer.

Example:

- Pharmacists see pharmacist-specific questions.
- Owners see owner-specific questions.
- Cleaners see field workflow questions.

## AI Interview Assistant

Instead of only forms, allow AI to ask follow-up questions:

```text
Respondent: The software has too many screens.
AI: What task were you trying to complete when that happened?
```

Human review still required.

## Response Clustering

Group similar pain points across responses:

- Outdates
- Screen bouncing
- Scheduling pain
- Training confusion
- Payment friction
- Compliance risk

## Backlog Candidate Generator

AI drafts backlog candidates from approved insights.

Human must approve before any ticket enters the product roadmap.

## Public Research Portal

Publish anonymized, human-approved research summaries later.

Do not publish raw responses.

## PDF Export

Allow internal export of completed responses as PDF.

## Training Module Builder

Use the same slide engine to build training modules and quizzes.

## SOP Builder

Use the same slide engine to build operational SOP walkthroughs.

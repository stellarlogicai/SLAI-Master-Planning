# User Flows

## Public Research Questionnaire Flow

```text
User visits SLAI website
↓
Clicks Research Forms
↓
Chooses product questionnaire
↓
Reads purpose/privacy slide
↓
Checks consent box
↓
Answers one question per slide
↓
Reviews answers optionally
↓
Submits response
↓
Sees thank-you screen
↓
SLAI receives structured response
```

## In-Person Interview Flow

```text
Jamie opens questionnaire on tablet/phone/laptop
↓
Asks respondent each question
↓
Types notes/answers into slide form
↓
Marks role/context
↓
Submits response
↓
Response becomes research data
```

## Beta Feedback Flow

```text
Beta tester uses product
↓
Tester opens product-specific feedback questionnaire
↓
Answers guided slides
↓
Submits bug/pain/friction feedback
↓
Admin dashboard flags recurring issues
↓
Human converts confirmed patterns into backlog items
```

## Training Reuse Flow

```text
Admin creates training module data
↓
Same slide engine renders training slides
↓
Employee moves through content
↓
Quiz slides collect answers
↓
Completion stored
↓
Manager sees training progress
```

## SOP Walkthrough Flow

```text
Employee is stuck during task
↓
Clicks SOP link
↓
Slide engine opens task-specific SOP
↓
Employee follows one step at a time
↓
Optional confirmation/checklist saved
```

# Supported Questionnaire Types

## ServicesOS Questionnaire

Target users:

- Cleaning business owners
- Cleaners
- Schedulers
- Admin staff
- Customers

Focus areas:

- Leads
- Estimates
- Contracts
- Deposits/final payments
- Scheduling
- Cleaner assignments
- Job checklists
- Photos/proof of work
- Customer communication
- Tips
- Training
- Recurring services

## Research Forms Index Page

The index page should show cards:

- ServicesOS Workflow Research
- EducationOS Workflow Research
- GrowthAI Workflow Research
- RetailOS / PharmacyOS Workflow Research
- FutureAI Research
- Pharmacy Workflow Research

Each card should include:

- Short description
- Estimated time
- Who should fill it out
- Start button
- Download PDF button if available

The form should be optimized for phone screens because many people may fill it out casually or during work breaks.

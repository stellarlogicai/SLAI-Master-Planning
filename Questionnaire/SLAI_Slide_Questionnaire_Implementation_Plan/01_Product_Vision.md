# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

SLAI should collect product research the same way it builds software: through clear workflows, structured data, human oversight, and reusable systems.

The slide questionnaire engine should make research feel less like paperwork and more like a guided conversation.

## Core Experience

A respondent lands on a questionnaire page and sees:

1. What the questionnaire is for.
2. What not to include.
3. A privacy/confidentiality reminder.
4. One question at a time.
5. A progress indicator.
6. A clear submit screen.

## User Feeling

The respondent should feel:

- Safe
- Not overwhelmed
- Guided
- Respected
- Clear on what is being asked
- Clear on what not to share

## SLAI Feeling

The internal SLAI team should receive:

- Structured answers
- Product area tags
- Respondent role/context
- Pain point summaries
- Patterns across responses
- Backlog candidates
- Human-reviewable insights

## Long-Term Vision

Eventually this system becomes a shared SLAI module:

```text
Questionnaire Engine
Training Engine
Quiz Engine
Onboarding Engine
Research Engine
SOP Walkthrough Engine
```

All powered by the same slide/content schema.

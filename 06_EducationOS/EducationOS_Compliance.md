# EducationOS Compliance

EducationOS handles sensitive student, parent, learning, and potentially medical-adjacent information.

## Sensitive Data
Student identity, guardian details, learning challenges, diagnosis-related notes, accommodations, attendance, progress, payments, emergency contacts, allergies for in-person students.

## Rules
AI never diagnoses. AI flags patterns for review. Human approval for official progress reports, certifications, accommodation recommendations, major plan changes, sensitive communications, and moderation decisions.

Customers should be able to export, access, and delete data when legally allowed.

# EducationOS AI Framework

## Philosophy
AI assists. Teachers decide. Parents understand. Students benefit.

## AI May
Suggest goals, draft lessons, suggest accommodations, create quizzes, draft worksheets, draft parent updates, summarize progress, identify patterns, recommend resources, generate study plans, assist marketplace tagging.

## AI Must Not
Diagnose students, replace teacher judgment, issue final certifications alone, hide uncertainty, or make high-impact decisions silently.

## Teacher Feedback Loop
AI suggests -> teacher reviews -> teacher adjusts -> system stores changes -> AI learns teacher preferences and student response patterns.

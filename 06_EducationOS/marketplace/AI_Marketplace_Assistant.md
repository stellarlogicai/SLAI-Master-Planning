# EducationOS AI Marketplace Assistant

AI helps creators publish better content faster.

## Capabilities
Content tagging, grade tagging, subject tagging, learning style tagging, difficulty scoring, price suggestions, descriptions, previews, categories, quality checks, similar item comparison.

## Workflow
Teacher saves lesson -> edits -> clicks publish -> AI suggests title, description, tags, grade, subject, price, target learning styles -> teacher approves -> content submitted.

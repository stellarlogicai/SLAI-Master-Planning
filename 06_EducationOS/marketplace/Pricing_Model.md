# EducationOS Marketplace Pricing Model

Fees depend on creator size and content type.

## Tier 1: Lightweight
Small: 10%  
Medium: 12%  
Large: 15%

## Tier 2: Interactive
Small: 12%  
Medium: 15%  
Large: 18%

## Tier 3: Video Courses
Small: 15%  
Medium: 20%  
Large: 25%

Avoid exceeding 25% unless costs are unusually high.

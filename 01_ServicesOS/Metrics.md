# ServicesOS Metrics

## Purpose

This document defines the metrics ServicesOS should track for product health, customer value, and SLAI growth.

Goal:

```text
Use data to prove ServicesOS is valuable and to know when SLAI is ready to grow.
```

---

# Jamie's Principle

```text
People can lie.
AI can lie.
Data does not lie.
```

Metrics should support decisions, not replace judgment.

---

# Stage 1 Goal

Before full launch, ServicesOS should prove:

```text
It works.
It saves time.
It reduces admin burden.
Customers will pay.
```

# Core Business Metrics

## MRR — Monthly Recurring Revenue

```text
Monthly recurring subscription revenue.
```

Target milestones:

- [ ] $100/month
- [ ] $250/month
- [ ] $500/month
- [ ] $1,000/month
- [ ] $2,500/month
- [ ] $5,000/month
- [ ] $10,000/month

---

## ARR — Annual Recurring Revenue

```text
MRR x 12
```

Useful for long-term tracking and investor conversations if needed.

---

## Customer Count

Track:

- Total companies
- Active companies
- Trial companies
- Paying companies
- Cancelled companies

---

## Churn

```text
Customers lost / customers at start of period
```

Track why customers leave:

- Too expensive
- Missing feature
- Too confusing
- Business closed
- Competitor
- Poor support
- Other

---

## ARPU — Average Revenue Per User/Company

```text
Monthly revenue / paying companies
```

Helps understand pricing.

# Metrics Rule

Do not track metrics just because you can.

Every metric should help answer one of these:

```text
Is the product useful?
Are customers getting value?
Is the company healthy?
Are employees supported?
Is SLAI ready to grow?
```

If a metric does not help answer those, do not prioritize it.

Employee Injury SOP

Category: Safety

Trigger
Employee reports injury
Step 1: Immediate Safety
□ Stop work
□ Assess severity
□ Call emergency services if required
Step 2: Documentation
□ Incident report
□ Photos if applicable
□ Witness statements
Step 3: Notification
□ Notify management
□ Notify HR if applicable
□ Notify insurance carrier if required
Step 4: Follow-Up
□ Workers compensation review
□ Return-to-work review
Completion Criteria
Incident fully documented and processed

# 00 — SLAI Company Engine Overview

**Status:** Future planning only  
**Active build priority:** ServicesOS remains priority one.  
**Purpose:** Create the blueprint for the internal company engine that helps SLAI operate, grow, and stay focused.

---

## Why This Exists

Jamie has many strong product ideas, but the current company priority is to stabilize ServicesOS and turn it into a real revenue-generating product. SLAI Company Engine is the long-term internal layer that helps make that easier.

The engine should help answer:

- What matters today?
- What is blocked?
- What product is priority one?
- What customer/beta feedback needs attention?
- What leads should be followed up with?
- What changed in ServicesOS, GrowthAI, or planning?
- What should Jamie do next?

---

## Core System

```text
SLAI Company Engine =
SLAI OS + GrowthAI + Research Agent + Client Finder + ServicesOS operating data
```

### ServicesOS

Creates the real operating data:

- Leads
- Customers
- Quotes
- Jobs
- Payments
- Service history
- Feedback
- Beta issues
- Metrics

### GrowthAI

Turns operating data and outside research into revenue opportunities:

- Leads to contact
- Follow-ups
- Referrals
- Cold lead recovery
- Customer value insights
- Service/vertical opportunities

### Research Agent

Collects outside intelligence:

- Business research
- Competitor notes
- Local market signals
- Industry patterns
- Potential customer context

### Client Finder

Finds and organizes good-fit prospects:

- Local businesses
- Service companies
- Vertical targets
- Contact-ready leads
- Fit scoring

### SLAI OS

Turns all of that into a command center:

- Founder dashboard
- Product status
- Blockers
- Alerts
- Handoffs
- Feedback
- Priorities
- Next actions

---

## Strategic Value

This is company-direct infrastructure. It supports SLAI itself before becoming customer-facing.

```text
Phase 1:
Help Jamie run SLAI and find customers.

Phase 2:
Help ServicesOS customers grow.

Phase 3:
Become a reusable intelligence layer across SLAI products.
```

---

## Design Principles

- ServicesOS remains the active build priority.
- SLAI Company Engine is planned deeply but built later.
- AI assists; humans approve important decisions.
- GrowthAI should never send outreach without human approval in early phases.
- SLAI OS should reduce founder chaos, not create more dashboards.
- Data should be linked from the start through shared IDs and events.
- Complexity is earned after real usage.

---

## Success Definition

The engine succeeds if it helps Jamie:

- Keep focus on ServicesOS
- Avoid losing context
- Track customer/beta feedback
- Know what to work on next
- Find and follow up with leads
- Turn operating data into business decisions
- Prepare future product expansion without rebuilding from scratch

---

This is a planning blueprint.

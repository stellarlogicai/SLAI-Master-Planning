# 02 — GrowthAI Revenue Engine

**Status:** Future planning only  
**Active build priority:** ServicesOS remains priority one.  
**Purpose:** Define GrowthAI as the revenue and customer-growth layer for SLAI first, then ServicesOS customers later.

---

## Core Role

GrowthAI should not be generic marketing AI.

It should be the revenue engine that helps SLAI and ServicesOS customers find, understand, follow up with, and convert good-fit customers.

```text
Phase 1:
Help Jamie/SLAI find customers.

Phase 2:
Help ServicesOS customers grow.

Phase 3:
Become a reusable growth intelligence layer across SLAI products.
```

---

## Phase 1: Help SLAI Directly

GrowthAI should first help Jamie find customers for ServicesOS.

Primary jobs:

- Find cleaning businesses
- Research business fit
- Identify likely pain points
- Draft outreach
- Track follow-ups
- Surface warm opportunities
- Suggest referrals
- Organize lead notes

Success is not automation. Success is helping Jamie take better sales actions with less mental load.

---

## Phase 2: Help ServicesOS Customers

After ServicesOS has customers, GrowthAI can become a customer-facing add-on.

Potential customer use cases:

- Follow up with inactive customers
- Ask for reviews
- Suggest recurring service reminders
- Identify high-value customers
- Recommend referral prompts
- Suggest seasonal campaigns
- Recover lost leads
- Track local growth opportunities

---

## Core Workflows

### Lead Discovery

```text
Find potential businesses
↓
Store lead record
↓
Score basic fit
↓
Send to research queue
```

### Lead Research

```text
Lead selected
↓
Research business context
↓
Identify likely pain points
↓
Summarize fit
↓
Suggest outreach angle
```

### Outreach Drafting

```text
Lead research complete
↓
AI drafts message
↓
Jamie reviews
↓
Jamie edits/approves
↓
Message sent manually or through approved workflow later
```

### Follow-Up Tracking

```text
Lead contacted
↓
Follow-up date set
↓
SLAI OS surfaces due follow-up
↓
GrowthAI drafts next message
↓
Jamie approves
```

## Data GrowthAI Needs

From ServicesOS later:

- Leads
- Quote requests
- Completed jobs
- Lost leads
- Customer value
- Service frequency
- Recurring schedules
- Reviews
- Customer feedback
- Business metrics

From external research later:

- Business name
- Location
- Website/social presence
- Service category
- Visible pain points
- Contact details
- Market signals

From Jamie/SLAI:

- Notes
- Call outcomes
- Follow-up dates
- Lead quality judgement
- Sales objections
- Customer fit decisions

---

## Human Approval Rule

GrowthAI must remain human-approved early.

```text
AI may research.
AI may draft.
AI may recommend.
Jamie approves outreach and important actions.
```

This protects the brand and keeps SLAI aligned with its philosophy: AI amplifies humans, not replaces them.

---

## GrowthAI Should Not Do Early

Avoid early overbuild:

- fully autonomous outreach
- mass spam campaigns
- complex ad platforms
- full CRM replacement
- scraping-heavy workflows
- automated sales decisions
- unapproved customer messages

The first version should be useful, controlled, and founder-friendly.

---

## Relationship to SLAI OS

GrowthAI creates revenue opportunities. SLAI OS shows Jamie which opportunities matter.

Example:

```text
GrowthAI:
Lead ABC has high fit and needs follow-up.

SLAI OS:
Shows ABC in today's priority panel.

Jamie:
Reviews and decides what to send.
```

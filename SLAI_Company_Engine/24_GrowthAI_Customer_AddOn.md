# 24 — GrowthAI Customer Add-On

**Status:** Future planning only  
**Active build priority:** ServicesOS remains priority one.  
**Purpose:** Define how GrowthAI can eventually become a customer-facing add-on for ServicesOS users after first helping SLAI internally.

---

## Core Positioning

GrowthAI should help SLAI first, then ServicesOS customers later.

```text
Phase 1:
GrowthAI helps Jamie/SLAI find customers.

Phase 2:
GrowthAI helps ServicesOS customers grow.
```

Do not make GrowthAI customer-facing before ServicesOS has real customer usage and enough operating data.

---

## Why This Matters

ServicesOS customers may need more than operations software. They may also need help growing their business.

GrowthAI can become the growth layer that helps them:

- follow up with leads
- recover lost quotes
- ask for reviews
- encourage referrals
- identify repeat-service opportunities
- understand which services perform best

---

## Customer-Facing Use Cases Later

### 1. Lost Lead Recovery

Detect leads or quotes that went cold.

Suggested action:

```text
Send friendly follow-up.
```

### 2. Review Requests

After a completed job, suggest asking for a review.

Suggested action:

```text
Draft a review request message.
```

### 3. Referral Prompts

Identify happy or repeat customers who may refer others.

Suggested action:

```text
Draft a referral request.
```

### 4. Recurring Service Suggestions

Identify customers who may benefit from recurring service.

Suggested action:

```text
Suggest monthly or biweekly service offer.
```

### 5. Seasonal Campaigns

Suggest campaigns based on timing and service type.

Examples:

- spring cleaning
- holiday cleaning
- move-out cleaning
- recurring maintenance
- lawn season
- vacation check-in season later

### 6. Customer Value Insights

Help the business understand:

- best customers
- repeat customers
- high-value service types
- service areas performing well
- customers at risk of going inactive

---

## Required ServicesOS Data Later

GrowthAI customer add-on may use:

- leads
- quotes
- jobs
- completed services
- customer feedback
- recurring schedules
- customer history
- payment status
- service type
- location/service area

Start with read-only recommendations.

---

## Human Approval Rule

For ServicesOS customers, the same human approval rule applies:

```text
AI suggests.
Business owner approves.
```

GrowthAI should not message a customer's clients without explicit business approval.

---

## MVP Add-On Later

First customer-facing version should be simple:

```text
Follow-up recommendations
Review request drafts
Referral request drafts
Recurring service suggestions
Customer value notes
Human approval workflow
```

---

## Not MVP

Do not start with:

- autonomous marketing campaigns
- ad buying
- mass emailing
- complex segmentation
- full CRM replacement
- external data enrichment at scale
- automatic customer messaging

---

## Relationship to SLAI OS

SLAI OS uses GrowthAI internally for SLAI.

ServicesOS customers later use GrowthAI as an add-on inside their business account.

```text
Internal GrowthAI teaches SLAI what works.
Customer-facing GrowthAI productizes the lessons later.
```

---

## Future Pricing Thought

GrowthAI could become:

- included basic recommendations
- paid add-on module
- usage-based AI feature
- premium growth package

Do not decide pricing until ServicesOS has real customers and GrowthAI value is proven.

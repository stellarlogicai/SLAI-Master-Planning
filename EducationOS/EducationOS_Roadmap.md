# EducationOS Roadmap

## Stage 1: Independent Tutors
Student CRM, parent CRM, scheduling, Stripe payments, attendance, notes, goals, AI lesson drafts, AI progress reports, parent portal.

## Stage 2: Tutoring Centers
Multiple tutors, permissions, group sessions, room scheduling, package billing, tutor metrics, admin dashboard.

## Stage 3: Homeschool Groups
Family accounts, multiple children, shared courses, curriculum tracking, family billing, group messaging.

## Stage 4: Private Schools
Teachers, classrooms, gradebooks, departments, school-wide reporting, report cards, transcripts.

## Stage 5: College / Adult Education
College tutoring, adult education, continuing education, certification prep.

## Stage 6: Trade Schools
Hands-on skills, safety modules, certification tracking, hours completed, instructor sign-off.

## Stage 7: Corporate Training
Employee training, SOPs, compliance, internal certifications, leadership development.

# EducationOS Creator Revenue

Creators should clearly see sale price, SLAI fee, Stripe/payment fees, creator payout, refund policy, and tax/reporting support.

## SLAI Provides
Hosting, discovery, AI tagging, descriptions, search, payments, moderation, analytics, support.

## Example
$10 worksheet bundle with 10% SLAI fee:
Sale price: $10  
SLAI fee: $1  
Creator share before payment fees: $9

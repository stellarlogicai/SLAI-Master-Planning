# EducationOS Marketplace Content Types

## Tier 1: Lightweight
Lesson plans, quizzes, worksheets, study guides, flash cards, homework, slides. Low storage/bandwidth.

## Tier 2: Interactive
Interactive lessons, custom learning plans, practice exams, AI simulations, adaptive modules. Medium compute/complexity.

## Tier 3: Video
Recorded courses, full curriculums, video libraries, workshops, professional development. Higher storage/bandwidth/CDN/moderation.

# EducationOS Vision

EducationOS exists to empower educators, support students, simplify learning administration, and create AI-assisted tools that enhance human teaching rather than replace it.

## Long-Term Scope
- Independent tutors
- Tutoring centers
- Homeschool groups
- Early childhood programs
- Private schools
- College tutoring
- Trade schools
- Adult education
- Corporate training
- Internal SLAI training

## Core Belief
Education is not one-size-fits-all. EducationOS must separate physical age, grade level, academic level, and developmental learning level.

## Human First AI
AI assists teachers, suggests plans, identifies patterns, drafts reports, and helps parents understand progress. AI never diagnoses, replaces teachers, or makes final high-impact decisions alone.

## Strategic Role
EducationOS is both a customer-facing product and SLAI's internal knowledge/training engine.

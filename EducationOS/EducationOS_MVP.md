# EducationOS MVP

## Target
Independent tutors and very small tutoring businesses.

## MVP Formats
One-on-one, small groups, virtual, hybrid.

## MVP Features
Student profiles, parent profiles, scheduling, attendance, session notes, learning goals, progress tracking, Stripe payments, parent portal, AI lesson drafts, AI progress report drafts, basic documents, basic marketplace draft/save.

## Not MVP
School gradebooks, large school administration, public school integrations, full video courses, corporate training, trade school certifications, advanced analytics, AI student simulator.

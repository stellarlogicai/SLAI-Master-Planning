# EducationOS Training Engine

EducationOS powers SLAI training across ServicesOS, RetailOS, PharmacyOS, SLAIOS, AI Research, customer onboarding, employee onboarding, and leadership development.

## Core Features
Course builder, lesson builder, slide builder, quiz builder, assignment builder, progress tracking, certifications, AI course generation, AI quiz generation, AI progress analysis, human approval workflows.

## Strategic Importance
EducationOS turns company knowledge into reusable training systems and helps preserve institutional knowledge.

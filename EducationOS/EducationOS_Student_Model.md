# EducationOS Student Model

## Core Fields
Student name, DOB, physical age, grade, academic level, developmental level, school, guardians, emergency contacts, learning style, subjects, strengths, weaknesses, goals, attendance, sessions, progress, teacher notes.

## Learning Level Separation
A student can be age 12, grade 6, reading level grade 2, and need younger developmental strategies.

## Diagnoses and Support Notes
EducationOS may store provided diagnosis-related information such as ADHD, dyslexia, autism, dyscalculia, anxiety, gifted needs, IEP/accommodation notes.

AI must never diagnose. AI may detect patterns and suggest that concerns be reviewed by a human.

## Allergies
Only needed for in-person students unless a tutor/school chooses otherwise.

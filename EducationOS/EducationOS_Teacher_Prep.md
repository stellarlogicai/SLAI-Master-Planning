# EducationOS Teacher Classroom Prep

## MVP Formats
One-on-one, small groups, virtual, hybrid.

## AI Teacher Prep Assistant
Teacher enters subject, age, learning level, group size, needs, time, and goals. AI generates lesson plan, activities, worksheets, quiz, backup activities, parent summary, and teacher notes.

## AI Student Simulator
Future module where AI simulates distracted, ADHD, dyslexic, gifted, shy, frustrated, or confused students so teachers can practice.

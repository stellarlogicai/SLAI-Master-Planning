# Privacy, Consent, and Safety

## Required Notice

Every questionnaire should start with a clear privacy/confidentiality notice.

Recommended text:

> This form is for general workflow and product research only. Do not include patient information, customer information, confidential employer data, private internal documents, screenshots, passwords, financial account details, or proprietary information.

## Required Checkbox

Before answering questions, respondents must confirm:

```text
[ ] I understand this form is for general workflow research only and I will not include confidential, patient, customer, employer, or proprietary information.
```

## Do Not Collect in V1

Avoid collecting:

- Patient information
- Prescription numbers
- Customer names
- Employer-confidential information
- Store identifiers
- Internal screenshots
- Passwords
- Account numbers
- Exact proprietary process documents
- Protected health information

## Optional Identity Fields

For public forms, keep identity optional:

- Name optional
- Email optional
- Role required
- Industry required
- Years of experience optional or range-based

## AI Review Safety

If AI later summarizes responses:

- AI should flag possible sensitive content.
- AI summaries should be human-reviewed before becoming backlog items.
- AI should not publish raw responses.
- AI should not infer private personal attributes.

## Human Review Rule

No user-submitted response should automatically become a product requirement without human review.

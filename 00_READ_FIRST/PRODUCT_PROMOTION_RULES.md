# Product Promotion Rules

A parked product can become active only if it passes at least one test:

1. It directly helps ServicesOS reach beta stability.
2. It directly helps ServicesOS get customers.
3. It directly reduces founder workload without creating major new complexity.
4. It directly supports revenue.
5. ServicesOS is already stable enough that the next product can safely begin.

Do not promote a parked product because it is exciting.

ServicesOS remains priority one.

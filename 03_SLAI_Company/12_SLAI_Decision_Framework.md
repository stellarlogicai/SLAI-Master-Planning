# SLAI Decision Framework

## Product Approval
1. Solve a real problem
2. Align with SLAI values
3. Reuse technology when possible
4. Help people
5. Be sustainable

## Build
- Behavioral Intelligence
- Core AI
- Morality AI
- Shared Memory

## Buy
- Payroll
- Accounting
- Legal tools

## Hybrid
- Cloud
- Payment processors
- AI APIs

## Investor Rules
Avoid investors initially. Maintain control. Protect employees and customers.

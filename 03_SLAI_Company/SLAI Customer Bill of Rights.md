# SLAI Customer Bill of Rights

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Our Promise

Customers choose SLAI because they trust us.

That trust is earned.

Every day.

---

# Transparent Pricing

Customers deserve:

* Clear pricing.
* Predictable costs.
* Easy to understand plans.

No surprises.

---

# No Hidden Fees

SLAI will never:

* Add surprise charges.
* Hide fees.
* Use misleading pricing.

Customers should know exactly what they are paying for.

---

# Customer Owns Their Data

Customer data belongs to:

The Customer.

Always.

Customers have the right to:

* Export their data.
* Delete their data.
* Access their data.
* Move to another provider.

SLAI never owns customer business information.

---

# AI Recommendations Explained

AI recommendations should:

* Explain why a decision was made.
* Show confidence when possible.
* Present alternatives.
* Remain understandable.

AI assists.

Humans decide.

---

# No Dark Patterns

SLAI will never:

* Hide cancellation buttons.
* Use deceptive interfaces.
* Manipulate customers into purchases.
* Create artificial urgency.

Trust matters more than short term conversions.

---

# Fair Cancellation Policies

Customers should stay because:

They love the product.

Not because:

Leaving is difficult.

Cancellation should:

* Be straightforward.
* Be fair.
* Preserve customer dignity.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Customers deserve:

* Reliable products.
* Honest communication.
* Responsive support.
* Continuous innovation.

---

# Final Promise

We want customers to say:

"SLAI helped my business succeed."

That matters more

than any marketing campaign,

valuation,

or revenue milestone.

Trust is earned.

And we intend to earn it every day.

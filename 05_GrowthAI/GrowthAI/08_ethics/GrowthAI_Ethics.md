# GrowthAI Ethics

## Core Rule

GrowthAI must never become a spam or manipulation engine.

## Rules

- Respect opt-outs.
- Do not fake personalization.
- Do not hide material facts.
- Do not mislead prospects.
- Do not pretend AI is human.
- Do not send automated outreach without approval in early phases.
- Do not pressure businesses unfairly.
- Do not exploit fear or desperation.
- Be honest about product maturity.

## AI Disclosure

If AI is used to draft messages internally, that does not necessarily need to be disclosed.

But GrowthAI should not create messages that falsely imply a human personally researched something they did not.

## Human Approval

Human approval required for:

- First outreach
- Sensitive messages
- Price offers
- Contract terms
- Claims about ROI
- Automated campaign launch

## SLAI Sales Philosophy

Do not sell to people the product cannot help.

A bad-fit customer is not a win.

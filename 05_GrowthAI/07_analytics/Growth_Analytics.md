# Growth Analytics

## Core Metrics

### Lead Metrics

- Leads added
- Leads researched
- Leads qualified
- Leads contacted
- Leads responded
- Demos booked
- Customers won
- Customers lost

### Conversion Metrics

- Lead to response rate
- Response to demo rate
- Demo to customer rate
- Trial to paid rate

### Channel Metrics

- Source
- Outreach channel
- Response rate
- Conversion rate

### Financial Metrics

- Customer acquisition cost
- Revenue added
- Monthly recurring revenue added
- Lifetime value estimate
- Referral credit cost

## Weekly Review Questions

- Which segment responded best?
- Which message worked best?
- What objection appeared most?
- Which leads should be prioritized next?
- What should be changed next week?

# ComplianceAI Overview

## Product Concept

ComplianceAI is a reusable SLAI product/module focused on keeping businesses ahead of required tasks, rules, deadlines, inspections, certifications, expirations, safety checks, and audit requirements.

It should not be limited to legal compliance. The broader purpose is operational compliance:

> Make sure required things are done correctly, on time, and with proof.

## Position in SLAI Ecosystem

```text
GrowthAI
→ Helps businesses find and grow customers.

EducationOS
→ Teaches people how to do work.

ComplianceAI
→ Makes sure required work actually gets done.

SLAIOS
→ Stores the memory, proof, and company knowledge.
```

## Core Workflow

```text
Object enters system
↓
Important data is captured
↓
Rules are attached
↓
Lifecycle is tracked
↓
Task is generated before action is due
↓
Employee gets clear instructions
↓
Manager verifies if required
↓
Audit trail is saved
```

## What ComplianceAI Tracks

- Product expiration dates
- Department pull windows
- Temperature checks
- Equipment inspections
- Vehicle maintenance
- Employee certifications
- Training renewals
- Policy acknowledgements
- Safety checks
- Hazardous waste handling
- Recall workflows
- Inventory routing
- Product batches
- Proposal expiration dates
- Follow-up deadlines
- Regulatory task schedules

## Human Oversight

AI can assist by summarizing trends, flagging overdue items, detecting recurring failures, suggesting schedules, explaining rules, drafting reports, and identifying risk areas.

AI should not override company policy, safety rules, legal requirements, pharmacist authority, manager authority, or human approval gates.

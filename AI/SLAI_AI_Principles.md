# SLAI AI Principles

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Human First AI

Artificial Intelligence exists to:

Enhance Humans.

Not Replace Humans.

---

SLAI builds AI that:

* Improves productivity.
* Supports decision making.
* Increases creativity.
* Helps people perform better.

---

# Human Oversight

Humans remain responsible.

AI recommendations:

* Can be questioned.
* Can be overridden.
* Should be understandable.

Humans make final decisions.

---

# Explainable AI

Whenever possible:

AI should explain:

* Why a recommendation was made.
* Confidence levels.
* Alternative options.

Customers deserve to understand:

How AI affects their business.

---

# Customer Data Rights

Customer data belongs to:

The Customer.

Always.

---

SLAI will never:

* Sell customer data.
* Share customer data without permission.
* Lock customers into proprietary formats.

---

Customers have the right to:

* Export their data.
* Delete their data.
* Access their data.

---

# Transparency

AI should never:

* Manipulate users.
* Mislead customers.
* Pretend certainty where none exists.

Trust is more important

than

short term gains.

---

# Ethical Development

Before building:

Ask:

* Does this help people?
* Does this reduce unnecessary work?
* Does this respect privacy?
* Could this harm customers?
* Could this harm employees?

If the answer is unclear:

Proceed carefully.

---

# Long Term Vision

SLAI believes:

The future is:

Humans

AI

Not:

Humans

↓

# SLAI AI Research Principles

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Purpose

The SLAI AI Research Division exists to:

Challenge assumptions.

Question accepted ideas.

Experiment relentlessly.

Push Artificial Intelligence forward.

---

The goal is not:

To follow trends.

The goal is:

To create them.

---

# Curiosity Over Credentials

Degrees are respected.

Experience is respected.

But curiosity matters more.

---

SLAI values:

* Curiosity
* Creativity
* Persistence
* Original Thinking
* Independent Research
* Intellectual Honesty

More than:

* Prestige
* Famous Companies
* Expensive Degrees

---

The question is never:

"Where did you work?"

The question is:

"What do you think nobody else sees?"

---

# Challenge Everything

No assumption

is above questioning.

---

Ask:

Why is it done this way?

What assumptions exist?

What happens if we reverse it?

What if everyone is wrong?

---

Accepted best practice

is not

accepted truth.

---

# Reward Crazy Ideas

The phrase:

"That's stupid."

Has no place at SLAI.

---

Instead ask:

Interesting.

Can we test it?

Can we measure it?

Can we prove it?

---

Many breakthroughs:

Sound ridiculous

before they become obvious.

---

# Failure Is Expected

Research is exploration.

Most experiments will fail.

That is expected.

---

If:

100 Experiments

↓

95 Fail

↓

5 Change Everything

That is success.

---

Failure is only unacceptable when:

* Nothing is learned.
* Results are hidden.
* Curiosity dies.

---

# Diverse Backgrounds

SLAI values unconventional thinkers.

Researchers may come from:

* Physics
* Psychology
* Neuroscience
* Game Development
* Biology
* Mathematics
* Agriculture
* Robotics
* Music Theory
* Self Taught Backgrounds

---

Different experiences

create

different perspectives.

---

Different perspectives

create

breakthroughs.

---

# Research Time

Researchers should have:

10-20%

Dedicated Research Time

---

This time exists for:

* Side Projects
* Experiments
* Papers
* Novel Architectures
* New Ideas

---

Innovation cannot exist

if every minute

must justify itself immediately.

---

# Intellectual Honesty

Results matter.

Even when:

* They prove us wrong.
* They destroy favorite ideas.
* They challenge assumptions.

---

Data tells the truth.

Not ego.

---

# Long Term Vision

SLAI does not want to:

Build another AI product.

---

SLAI wants to:

Redefine

how AI is built.

---

The world does not change

because people agree.

The world changes

because someone asks:

"What if everyone is wrong?"

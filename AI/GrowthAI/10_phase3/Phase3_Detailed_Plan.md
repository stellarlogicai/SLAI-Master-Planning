# GrowthAI Phase 3 Detailed Plan

## Timeline

3-6 months after MVP, once ServicesOS has real customers and GrowthAI has enough feedback data.

## Phase 3 Goals

- Semi-automated lead discovery
- Industry-specific playbooks
- Campaign recommendations
- Memory-based learning
- Cross-product growth support

## Features

### Automated Lead Discovery

AI-assisted discovery from public sources where compliant.

### Industry Playbooks

Playbooks for:

- Cleaning
- Tutoring
- Retail
- Pharmacy
- Greenhouse
- Future verticals

### Growth Forecasting

Estimate:

- Number of leads needed
- Demos needed
- Expected customers
- Expected revenue

### Cross-Product Use

GrowthAI should grow:

- ServicesOS
- EducationOS
- RetailOS
- PharmacyOS
- Future SLAI products

## Human Approval

Even in Phase 3, major outreach campaigns require human approval.

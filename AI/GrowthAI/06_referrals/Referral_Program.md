# GrowthAI Referral Program

## Purpose

Reward customers who help SLAI grow.

## Reward Options

Customers can choose:

- Billing credits
- AI credits
- Marketplace credits
- Percentage discount
- Future feature credits

## Suggested MVP Rewards

### Successful Referral

Customer refers a business that becomes a paying customer.

Reward options:

- $100 billing credit
- Equivalent AI credits
- 5-10% off next invoice
- Marketplace credit when EducationOS marketplace exists

## Rules

- Reward issued after referred customer pays first invoice.
- Referrer chooses reward type.
- Rewards are tracked in GrowthAI.
- Fraud prevention required long term.

## Why Choice Matters

Some customers value cash savings.

Some value AI usage.

Some value training/marketplace content.

Letting customers choose increases perceived value.

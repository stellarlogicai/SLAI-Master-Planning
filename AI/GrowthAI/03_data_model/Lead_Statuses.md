# GrowthAI Lead Statuses

## Status List

### New

Lead was added but not researched.

### Research Needed

Lead has enough basic info to research.

### Research Complete

AI or human research has created a summary.

### Outreach Drafted

AI drafted outreach but human has not sent it.

### Contacted

Lead has received first outreach.

### Responded

Lead has replied.

### Interested

Lead expressed interest.

### Demo Scheduled

Meeting/demo scheduled.

### Trial

Lead is testing ServicesOS.

### Customer

Lead converted to paying customer.

### Lost

Lead declined or went cold.

### Do Not Contact

Lead opted out or should not be contacted.

## Rules

- Do Not Contact must override all other statuses.
- Lost leads can be re-opened only manually.
- Customer leads should convert into CRM/customer records.

# GrowthAI MVP Requirements

## MVP Goal

Build the minimum useful internal tool that helps Jamie start finding ServicesOS customers after wife beta testing.

The MVP does not need autonomous lead scraping or automatic sending.

It needs to help Jamie:

1. Store leads
2. Research leads
3. Score leads manually or with AI assistance
4. Draft outreach
5. Track communication
6. Track pipeline status
7. Review weekly progress

## MVP Timeline

Target build time: 2-4 weeks.

## MVP Must-Have Features

### Lead Database

Each lead should store:

- Business name
- Owner/contact name
- Email
- Phone
- Website
- Location
- Industry
- Business size estimate
- Source
- Status
- Tags
- Notes
- AI research summary
- Pain points
- Fit score
- Outreach angle
- Created date
- Updated date

### Lead Status Pipeline

Initial statuses:

- New
- Research Needed
- Research Complete
- Outreach Drafted
- Contacted
- Responded
- Interested
- Demo Scheduled
- Trial
- Customer
- Lost
- Do Not Contact

### AI Research

Given website/review/social notes, AI should produce:

- Business summary
- Likely pain points
- Possible ServicesOS fit
- Recommended outreach angle
- Confidence level
- Reasons for confidence
- Missing information

### AI Outreach Drafting

Generate:

- Cold email
- LinkedIn message
- Call script
- Follow-up message
- Demo preparation notes

### Human Approval

No message should send automatically in MVP.

Jamie reviews, edits, and sends.

### Dashboard

Dashboard should show:

- Lead counts by status
- Top leads by score
- Follow-ups due
- Conversion metrics
- Referral credits
- Weekly review summary

## MVP Out of Scope

Do not build yet:

- Fully automated lead scraping
- Automatic email sending
- Autonomous campaigns
- Complex CRM integrations
- Paid ad integrations
- Multi-user sales team features
- Full standalone SaaS billing
- Complex machine learning scoring

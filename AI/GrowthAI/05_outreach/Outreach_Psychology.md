# Outreach Psychology

## Purpose

GrowthAI should help humans write better outreach, not spam people.

## Principles

### Be Direct

Busy owners do not want long paragraphs.

### Be Relevant

Mention a specific reason they might benefit.

### Be Human

A little warmth or humor can help.

Example inspired by real outreach:

```text
Yes? No? Kick rocks?
```

This works because it is low-pressure and human.

### Do Not Fake Personalization

Never pretend to know something that is not known.

### Respect Timing

Do not hammer leads repeatedly.

### Know When to Stop

If someone does not respond after a reasonable sequence or opts out, stop.

## Recommended Sequence

1. Initial short message
2. Friendly follow-up
3. Value-focused follow-up
4. Final permission-based check-in

## Avoid

- Manipulation
- Fake urgency
- Misleading claims
- Overpromising
- AI pretending to be human

# GrowthAI Phase 2 Detailed Plan

## Timeline

1-2 months after MVP proves useful.

## Phase 2 Goals

- Improve prioritization
- Add follow-up automation suggestions
- Track communication history
- Add referral tracking
- Add campaign analytics

## Features

### AI Lead Scoring

Lead score based on fit and conversion likelihood.

### Follow-Up Suggestions

AI recommends follow-up timing and message type.

### Communication History

Store all interactions and outcomes.

### Referral Program

Track referrals and reward selection.

### Campaign Tracking

Group leads by campaign.

Examples:

- Local cleaning businesses
- Residential cleaners
- Cleaning businesses with outdated websites
- Facebook group leads

## Acceptance Criteria

- Jamie can see follow-ups due.
- AI can recommend which leads to prioritize.
- Communication history is preserved.
- Referral rewards are tracked.
- Weekly analytics are useful.

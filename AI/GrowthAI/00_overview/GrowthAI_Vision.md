# GrowthAI Vision

## Purpose

GrowthAI exists to help SLAI find, research, prioritize, contact, and convert potential customers.

It should help answer:

- Who should we contact?
- Why are they a fit?
- What problems might they have?
- What should we say?
- When should we follow up?
- Which channels are working?
- Which industries convert best?
- Which customers refer others?
- What should we learn from wins and losses?

## Initial Product Focus

The first target is ServicesOS customer acquisition after wife beta testing and critical bug fixes.

Initial target customers:

- Small cleaning businesses
- Residential cleaning companies
- Cleaning companies with 1-20 employees
- Cleaning businesses using disconnected tools
- Cleaning businesses with weak online booking, weak training, or manual scheduling

## Long-Term Product Focus

GrowthAI should eventually support all SLAI products:

- ServicesOS
- EducationOS
- RetailOS
- PharmacyOS
- GreenhouseOS
- SLAIOS
- FutureAI products

## Product Evolution

### Stage 1: Jamie's Internal Sales Assistant

Manual lead entry, AI research, AI outreach drafts, founder review.

### Stage 2: SLAI Growth Engine

Lead scoring, follow-up automation, referral tracking, campaign analytics, memory.

### Stage 3: Standalone SaaS

Businesses use GrowthAI to find and convert customers in their own industries.

## Human First Sales Philosophy

GrowthAI must:

- Respect customers
- Avoid spam
- Avoid manipulation
- Avoid fake personalization
- Be honest about limitations
- Help humans communicate better
- Keep human approval for outreach until trust is earned

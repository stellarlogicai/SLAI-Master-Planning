# Customer Facing AI

Purpose:

Human interaction.

Responsibilities:

* Conversation
* Empathy
* Intent Recognition
* Explanation
* Clarification

---

It does NOT:

* Make important decisions.
* Access everything directly.
* Override humans.

---

It acts as:

Translator

between

Humans

and

The Intelligence Ecosystem.

---

# Coordinator / Executive AI

Purpose:

Decision Making.

---

Responsibilities:

* Route requests.
* Ask specialists.
* Retrieve memory.
* Query morality system.
* Weigh evidence.
* Explain decisions.
* Escalate to humans.

---

The Coordinator does not know everything.

It knows:

WHO to ask.

WHEN to ask.

HOW to combine answers.

---

Recommendations.

Confidence.

# Shared Memory

Shared Memory is:

The connective tissue.

---

Stores:

* Facts
* Customer History
* Past Conversations
* Decisions
* Outcomes
* Values
* Patterns
* Long Term Context

---

Purpose:

Allow many minds

to behave

as one.

---

Without Memory:

Every specialist starts over.

---

With Memory:

Knowledge compounds.

---

# Morality / Values AI

Purpose:

Protect company values.

---

Checks decisions against:

* Founder Handbook
* Leadership Principles
* Customer Bill of Rights
* Employee Compensation Philosophy
* Human Feedback
* Historical Outcomes

---

Responsibilities:

* Flag ethical concerns.
* Explain tradeoffs.
* Detect conflicts.
* Encourage long term thinking.

---

Morality AI does NOT:

Determine absolute truth.

Humans remain responsible.

# Cross-OS Use Cases

## ServicesOS

ComplianceAI can support cleaning chemical expiration tracking, PPE restock/expiration, equipment filter replacement, vehicle maintenance reminders, employee training renewals, safety inspection tasks, first aid kit checks, and customer-specific compliance requirements.

## RetailOS

ComplianceAI can support outdates, department pull rules, RGM routing, toxic/hazardous tote routing, recalls, temperature checks, store walks, safety checks, equipment checks, and waste tracking.

## PharmacyOS

ComplianceAI can support pharmacy fridge monitoring, vaccine fridge monitoring, OTC outdates, RX inventory checks, compliance logs, pharmacist verification tasks, hazardous disposal rules, recall handling, and temperature corrective action logs.

Important: PharmacyOS must keep stronger human approval and pharmacist oversight where required.

## GreenhouseOS

ComplianceAI can support nutrient expiration, pesticide compliance, treatment logs, harvest batch records, cure timelines, plant disposal records, equipment cleaning logs, environmental checks, and batch recall readiness.

## EducationOS

ComplianceAI can support tutor certifications, student forms, parent agreements, course completion deadlines, required training renewals, safety acknowledgements, and policy updates.

## GrowthAI

ComplianceAI can support follow-up deadlines, proposal expiration, referral reward deadlines, campaign review dates, outreach compliance rules, consent/opt-out tracking, and human approval gates.

## SLAIOS

ComplianceAI can support employee policy acknowledgements, device audits, security reviews, access reviews, internal certification renewal, incident response follow-ups, and leadership review deadlines.

## Key Pattern

```text
Track the object.
Attach rules.
Generate tasks.
Guide action.
Verify completion.
Store proof.
```
